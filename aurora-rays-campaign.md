## 🔗 AUTOMATION WORKFLOWS (AutoStack)

1. **Ad Click → Landing Page A/B** — Route traffic to best-converting variant
2. **Form Submit → Lead Nurture** — Trigger 7-email audit sequence instantly
3. **Demo Booked → Confirmation + Prep** — Send confirmation + 3 prep emails
4. **No-Show → Reschedule Flow** — Auto-fire reschedule sequence after 1 hour
5. **High Score Lead → Sales Alert** — Score > 80 → notify sales + create CRM deal
6. **Client Won → Onboarding** — Trigger onboarding sequence + setup checklist
7. **Starter → Growth Upsell** — After 30 days on Starter → trigger upsell sequence
8. **ROAS > 5× → Scale Budget** — Auto-increase winning campaign budget 25%
9. **CTR < 2% → Pause + Alert** — Pause underperformer + queue new creative
10. **Inactive 14 Days → Re-engage** — Fire win-back sequence for cold leads
